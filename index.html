TOOLING PILLAR

Core Tooling pillar is responsible to ensure that various tools being used by R&D for managing and delivering the change are accessible and available at any given point. Major responsibilities areas are :

  • Salesforce and JIRA Integration Ownership.

  • Co-ordination across teams to ensure availability of Salesforce and JIRA.

  • Program management for major activities around version upgrades, etc,.

  • JIRA Plugin Maintenance.

  • Managing and tracking the licensing of Confluence, PowerBI, MIRO, Smartsheet, etc,.
